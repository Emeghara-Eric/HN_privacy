HealthNode Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 24/11/2025

1. Introduction

HealthNode (“the App”) is a personal women’s health companion designed to help users track menstrual cycles, predict ovulation, and log symptoms. This Privacy Policy explains how HealthNode collects, uses, and protects your information.

2. Data Collection and Usage (Version 1.0.0)

The current version of HealthNode does not require users to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device.

Stored locally on your phone:

  • Period logs
  • Cycle dates
  • Symptoms
  • Predictions & insights

3. No Personal Data Collection

HealthNode does not collect or process:

  • Names or contact details
  • Location information
  • Device identifiers
  • Financial information
  • Photos, media, or files
  • Health data outside what you manually enter

4. No Data Sharing or Selling

HealthNode does not share your data with third parties, sell any user data, or use your information for advertising or analytics. All information remains solely on your device.

5. Permissions Used

The MVP version of HealthNode does not request any sensitive permissions. If future updates require additional permissions, they will be clearly disclosed in-app and on this page.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your device controls the security of your information. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data.

7. Future Updates (Cloud Storage & Login)

Future versions of HealthNode may introduce:

  • Optional user accounts
  • Secure cloud backups
  • Authentication features

If these features are added, this Privacy Policy will be updated. User privacy and data protection will remain a priority, and no information will be shared or sold.

8. Children’s Privacy

HealthNode is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or store information from users under 13.
